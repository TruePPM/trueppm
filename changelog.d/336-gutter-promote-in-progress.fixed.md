Schedule view: dragging a task from the **Unscheduled** gutter onto today's column on the timeline now transitions the card from **To Do** to **In Progress** on the Board, matching the unified data model across agile and waterfall views. Future drops keep the card at **To Do** so the board doesn't claim work has begun for tasks scheduled weeks or months out, and past drops pin `actual_start` to the chosen `planned_start` so the historical date isn't overwritten by the backend's auto-`actual_start = today` (#336).
