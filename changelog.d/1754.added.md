- **"+ Phase" schedule action**: the Schedule/Gantt toolbar gets a first-class `+ Phase` button (⌘P / Ctrl+P) alongside `+ Task` and `+ Milestone`. It inserts a new WBS summary row and drops it into inline rename; a childless "phase-in-waiting" row shows a ghost "Add first task to this phase" affordance until it gains its first structural child. A phase is otherwise emergent, not a new task type — any summary task with a structural (non-subtask) child is automatically a phase.
- **Phases excluded from contributor pickers**: a phase is never selectable in the quick-log time picker (top bar and My Work), never becomes a My Work row, and never shows an assignee control — matching the backend's rejection of assignment, time logging, and (per the sibling sprint-picker change) sprint membership on a phase.
