- **Personal settings pages now share one complete, consistent secondary nav.** All
  four `/me/settings/*` pages (General, Notifications, Connected accounts, Personal
  access tokens) render the same in-page subnav listing every page, so you can move
  freely between them instead of dead-ending once you leave General. The account menu
  now also lists Connected accounts, and a bare `/me/settings` URL redirects to
  General rather than showing "Page not found" (#2023).
