"Load demo data" — a one-click sample loader. A fresh install can load the **Atlas Platform Launch** demo (a three-project hybrid program spanning agile, waterfall, and hybrid delivery with cross-project dependencies, three-point estimates, baselines, risks, and a resource roster) from the empty Programs page, the `load_sample_project` management command, or `POST /api/v1/programs/load-sample/`. Sample programs are flagged and offer a one-click "Remove sample data" teardown.
