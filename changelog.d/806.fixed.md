Schedule view: right-click no longer freezes after deleting a row. Deleting a task (especially one on the critical path) used to orphan the row's context-menu portal when cache invalidation unmounted the row, blocking subsequent right-clicks on every other row until a full page refresh. The row now gets the in-flight treatment during delete, the open menu auto-closes, and the right-click handler is suppressed for the row being deleted.
