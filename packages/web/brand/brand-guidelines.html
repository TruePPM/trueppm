02 — Construction

Clear space & minimum size

Keep clear space equal to the diameter of the large terminal node on all sides. Never place the mark tighter than that, and never below the minimum legible sizes.

Wordmark spec

Set in Space Grotesk Bold (700) at −0.02em tracking. True in navy (or Reversed Ink on dark); PPM always in Truth Sage, set solid with no space before it. For print or unknown environments, outline the text (see wordmark.svg).

07 — Iconography

Line icons, 1.5px

Icons share the mark's drawing logic: 1.5px strokes, rounded caps and joins, on a 16/20/24 grid. Geometric, never decorative. Navy by default; sage only when an icon represents the path, an action, or a positive state.

09 — Motion & voice

Quick, precise, never showy

Motion tokens

--dur-fast120mshovers, toggles
--dur200msmost transitions
--dur-slow320mspanels, overlays
--ease-standardcubic-bezier(.2,.7,.2,1)

When the schedule re-levels, the critical path animates first and fastest — motion should reinforce causality, not decorate it.

Voice & tone

  • Precise. Say SV, CPI, P80 without apology. Numbers earn trust.
  • Plain-spoken. Short sentences. No PPM jargon for its own sake.
  • Opinionated. The schedule is the source of truth — and we'll say so.
  • Calm. We surface risk early so nobody has to panic late.
